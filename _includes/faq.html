What is the Self-organising University
The idea behind the University is to create a space for people who want to study together.
Where is the University?
We are currently talking to various venues in and around King's Cross in the NW1 and WC1 postcodes of London. We expect to find a home soon.
When does the University open?
We are going to open in September 2013, initially one night per week from 6pm to 8pm.
What does it cost?
There is no cost.
How do I join?
Just "sign up"http://www.meetup.com/selforganising/ and come along.
Can I organise my own study groups?
Please do. You do not have to ask. Just "schedule a new meetup":http://www.meetup.com/selforganising/.
Does the study group have to be for an online course?
No. In the spirit of Ivan Illich, we invite anyone "to transform each moment of his [or her] living into one of learning, sharing, and caring.":http://ournature.org/~novembre/illich/1970_deschooling.html
How do I get involved in the development of the University?
There is a monthly planning meeting that is open to anyone currently attending (or between) course meetups.
Who is behind the University?
I am Dan Sofer, a bit of a "MOOC":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massive_open_online_course enthusiast, who hated school and loves learning, especially with other people. I fit a bit of freelance software development in between studies.
