The Self-organising Manifesto

The resources now becoming available online make it possible for the first time to imagine a world of self-organised and life-long study for all of us.

Learning is a social activity. To make full use of this revolution in learning we need mutual support.

The Self-organising University brings people together in their self-directed studies, as study buddies, in study groups, and using the idea of peer matching, in the spirit of Ivan Illich's De-schooling Society.
