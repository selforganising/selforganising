The Self-organising Manifesto

My children have had an experience of education almost indistinguishable from my own.* The education my grandchildren make for themselves will be utterly different.

The resources now becoming available online make it possible for the first time to imagine a world of self-organised study, not just for a few, but for all of us.

But learning is a social activity and to make full use of this revolution in learning, we need to look for mutual support.

The Self-organising University is a social network for people who want to study. It is here to help people join together to take control of their own life-long education.
