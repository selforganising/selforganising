Suggested copy for Collective website

Learn to code at the Collective Academy

Who is this for?
Anyone who wants to learn how to code, although we are giving priority to Camden residents and ex-pupils of Camden schools. Places are limited. We recommend that you apply soon.
How long will this course last?
It is a 6-week full-time course.
What previous experience do I need?
No previous experience is required.
Will it be hard?
Yes. You may have to work harder than you have ever worked before.
What will I learn?
You will learn the fundamentals of coding and you will learn how to build web sites and web applications.You will learn about HTML and CSS and you will learn to program in Javascript and in Python. You will learn about development tools, hosting environments, source control and integrating 3rd party apps into your website.
Who will be teaching this course?
You wil be using some of the best-available online learning resources and you will be guided by a long-time industry veteran who has been writing software since 1985, has been building websites since 1995 and has worked on web projects for, among others, The Guardian, the BBC and the London 2012 Olympic games.
Will I get a chance to work on real projects?
You will. We are collaborating with Collective members and local businesses to line up real projects for you to work on.
Will I be able to get work as a web developer at the end of the course?
We absolutely cannot guarantee it, but if you are able to successfully complete the course you will be well on the way to acquiring the skills you need either to seek employment as a developer or to work on your own projects.
Will there be any follow up courses?
Yes, This is a pilot course, but as long as there is demand and we can find the funding, we will run this course again. We will also be running a series of advanced courses if there is the demand for it.
How much will it cost?
We saved the best bit for last. It's free.
